How to make a donation

If you’re interested to donate, the process is very simple: click the ‘Make a donation’ button and fill in donation form

You can donate money, food, toys, drugs and other things. ChangeLives address is Filimon Street 81, Bucharest.

From drugs category you can donate only parasite prevention drugs and vaccines for the following diseases: canine distemper, canine hepatitis, canine parainfluenza, heartworm, lyme disease and parvovirus

Your help really matters! Thank you!
